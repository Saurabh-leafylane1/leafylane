Recalls and Product Safety

At LeafyLane, your safety and plant experience are our top priorities. We ensure every product meets quality, safety, and sustainability standards. However, in rare cases, certain products may be recalled due to safety concerns or quality issues.

Latest Notice (March 2025):
We have voluntarily recalled a batch of ceramic plant pots (SKU: CP-221) due to potential cracking issues under high moisture. Customers who purchased this item will be contacted directly and offered a free replacement or refund.

What to Do If You Received a Recalled Product:

  • Check your order history and confirm the affected product SKU
  • Stop using the product immediately
  • Contact our support team through your account or email support@leafylane.com
  • We will initiate a return pickup and process your refund or replacement

We deeply apologize for the inconvenience and assure you that we are constantly improving our quality assurance process to prevent future issues.

For more updates and recall information, visit our Product Safety Updates page.
