Returns Centre

We want you to love your LeafyLane plants and products. If something isn’t right, we’re here to help you with easy returns and exchanges.

  • Return Window: You can request a return within 7 days of delivery.
  • Returnable Items: Most plants, tools, and accessories are eligible for return if they are unused and in original condition.
  • Non-returnable: Customized planters, gift items, and plants with damage due to user mishandling.
  • Return Process:
    1. Log in to your account and go to My Orders.
    2. Select the item you want to return.
    3. Click on "Return" and choose a reason.
    4. Schedule a pickup or drop off at the nearest LeafyLane hub.
  • Refunds: Once your return is verified, the refund will be processed within 5–7 business days to your original payment method.
  • Need Help? Email us at support@leafylane.com or use our chat support.
